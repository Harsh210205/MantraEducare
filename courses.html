Our Courses

At Mantra EduCare School, we offer age-appropriate and engaging educational programs designed to support early childhood development and prepare children for the future.

Play Group

Our Play Group program is designed for toddlers aged 2 to 3 years. We focus on developing social interaction, basic motor skills, and communication through fun-filled activities like storytelling, music, free play, and art & craft.

Nursery

For children aged 3 to 4 years, our Nursery program blends foundational learning with play. Children are introduced to alphabets, numbers, colors, shapes, and other pre-academic skills while fostering curiosity and imagination.

Senior (LKG & UKG)

In the Senior class (LKG & UKG), children aged 4 to 6 years engage in structured learning. Our curriculum focuses on language development, early math, environmental awareness, life skills, and preparation for primary schooling.
