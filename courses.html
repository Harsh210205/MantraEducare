Our Courses

Play Group

Designed for children aged 2 to 3 years, our Play Group nurtures curiosity, motor skills, and social interaction through stories, play, and song-based learning in a safe and loving environment.

Junior KG

For ages 3 to 4, Junior KG introduces foundational concepts in language, numbers, and colors using interactive games, phonics, craft activities, and early writing practice.

Senior KG

Tailored for 4 to 5-year-olds, Senior KG prepares students for primary school with structured learning in literacy, numeracy, science, and theme-based projects.
